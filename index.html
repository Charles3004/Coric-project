Our Mission

At The City of Refuge, our mission is to reveal the power of God as the ultimate solution to the challenges of our generation. We are committed to creating an atmosphere where divine encounters ignite transformation, restore destinies, and dismantle the agenda of the enemy. We believe that no life is beyond the touch of God, and every genuine encounter with Him is a life-altering experience. Through relentless pursuit of God’s presence, we press into dimensions of His power beyond expression, leading people to experience real change — not over time, but through God’s timely intervention. No matter how far anyone has strayed, we declare boldly: God is waiting at the place of your decision. Come and experience God at work.
